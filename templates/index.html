1. The Logic

⚠️ ORDER DOES NOT MATTER

The feedback dots are shuffled. They do NOT correspond to the position of the numbers in your guess.

BULL: Correct Number in one of the given Spots.
COW: Correct Number, but in the Wrong Spot.
GRAY: This Number is NOT in the code.
HOLLOW: Waiting for your next input.

2. Tools

  • 🕵️ Detective: Right-Click pad to strike out.
  • 💡 Hint: Reveals one correct digit.
  • 🏳️ Surrender: Reveal code.

3. Multiplayer Mission

  • Race to Crack: Be the first to solve the code.
  • Real-time Intel: Watch opponent attempts on their card.
  • Chat: Coordinate or distract via the secure channel.
  • Surrender: Use 🏳️ for a tactical forfeit.

4. Special Modes

  • ⚡ Timer: 3 Minutes to crack the code.
  • ♾️ Unlimited: Endless guesses. Rows appear as you need them.
